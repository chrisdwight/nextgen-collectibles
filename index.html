Card Grading Services

What is Card Grading?

Card grading is the process of having your trading cards professionally evaluated for authenticity and condition. Graded cards are sealed in tamper-proof cases and assigned a grade, which can significantly increase their value and desirability.

How Does It Work?
  1. Submit your card(s) to a certified grading service through our website or at one of our events.
  2. Experts inspect your card for centering, corners, edges, and surface quality.
  3. Your card is assigned a grade (usually 1â€“10) and sealed in a protective case with a certification label.
  4. Receive your graded card back, ready for display or sale!

Why grade your cards? Grading protects your investment, increases resale value, and gives buyers confidence in authenticity and condition.
